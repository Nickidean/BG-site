FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about switching and our tariffs.

What is the best energy tariff for me? +
It depends on your usage and how much certainty you want over your bills. A fixed tariff protects you from price rises, while a variable tariff offers flexibility with no exit fees.
How long does it take to switch to British Gas? +
Switching is straightforward and usually takes around 21 days. We handle everything — you don't need to contact your current supplier.
Will I need a smart meter? +
We'll install a smart meter for free as part of your switch. It lets you track your energy use in real time and sends automatic meter readings so your bills are always accurate.
What is PeakSave and how does it work? +
PeakSave is a smart feature that rewards you for reducing electricity use during peak demand periods. You can earn credits or lower rates by shifting usage to off-peak hours like overnight.
Is 100% renewable electricity really green? +
Our green tariffs match every unit of electricity you use with renewable energy fed into the National Grid from solar, wind, and hydro sources. We also offset the carbon from gas usage.
