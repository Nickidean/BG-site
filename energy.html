FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about switching.

What is the best energy tariff for me?+
It depends on how much certainty you want over your bills. A fixed tariff protects you from price rises; a variable tariff offers flexibility with no exit fees.
How long does switching take?+
Switching usually takes around 21 days. We handle everything — you don’t need to contact your current supplier.
Will I need a smart meter?+
We’ll install a smart meter for free. It sends automatic readings so your bills are always accurate, and you can track usage in real time.
What is PeakSave?+
PeakSave rewards you for reducing electricity use during peak demand periods. Shift usage to off-peak hours and earn savings automatically.
Is 100% renewable electricity really green?+
Our green tariffs match every unit of electricity you use with renewable energy fed into the National Grid from solar, wind, and hydro sources.
